About Me

I am REN Siyu, a joint PhD candidate at Tianjin University and City University of Hong Kong. During my doctoral studies at Tianjin University, I have had the privilege of joining Prof. HOU Junhui's research group, where I have been able to deepen my knowledge and contribute to cutting-edge research. My research interests lie in Geometry Processing and Computer Graphics, with a particular focus on Implicit Geometry Representation.

Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Tianjin University in 2018. During my studies, I have published multiple papers in top-tier conferences and journals.
